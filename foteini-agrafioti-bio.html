Dr. Foteini Agrafioti

Chief Science Officer, RBC

Foteini is the Chief Science Officer at RBC and Head of RBC Research, the R&D arm of the bank that performs fundamental research in machine learning. Prior to that, Foteini was the CIO at Architech, She also founded and served as CTO at Nymi, a biometrics security company and maker of the Nymi wristband.

Foteini is an inventor of HeartID, the first biometric technology to authenticate users based on their unique cardiac rhythms. She is a TED speaker and serves on the editorial review boards of several scientific journals. Foteini was named “Inventor of the Year” in 2012 at the University of Toronto where she received the PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering.
