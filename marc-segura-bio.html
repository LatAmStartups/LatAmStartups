Marc Segura

CEO, Play Business

Founder and CEO of Play Business, first equity crowd funding company in Mexico. Marc received a B.A. in Law and Economics from the Tecnologico de Monterrey, the most recognized entrepreneurial university in Mexico where he currently acts an active professor. He is passionate about startups and totally committed with Play Business. Currently he is an investor in ten startups, part of the board in three of them and an active member of AFICO (Asociación de Fondeo Colectivo en México) in charge of all the legal aspects.
