Israel Pons

CEO, Angels Nest Mexico

Israel Pons is CEO, partner and co-founder at Angels Nest Mexico.

Angels Den Mexico has over 120 angel investors, with strong ties to government and industry in Mexico.

Israel previously worked in the Uk for 23 years, where he served as the head of operations for the number one entertainment company in that country. He also founded four companies in the UK and successfully sold three of them.

He frequently serves as mentor for numerous startups in Mexico, and oftens speaks at conferences in the UK and Mexico.
