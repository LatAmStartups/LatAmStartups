Senior Business Advisor, ICT

The Ministry is responsible for attracting and retaining business in the Province of Ontario, this is pursued through marketing the Province of Ontario to businesses and investors from around the globe.

His focus has been in the Information and Communications Technology sector as well as Financial Services, Fintech and Cybersecurity. Peter has over 20 years of experience in Technology Sales and Marketing and has worked for companies from around the globe, with a focus on Silicon Valley, CA and Dallas, TX. He has worked as a Manufacturers Representative, Sales Manager, Consultant and Country Manager for technology companies of all sizes. Peter was born and raised in Toronto attended both Humber College and Ryerson University studying Sales, Marketing and Media.
