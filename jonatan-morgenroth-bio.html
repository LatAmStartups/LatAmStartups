Jonatan Morgenroth

Director of Sales, LATAM, at TouchBistro

Jonatan Morgenroth is the Director of LATAM at TouchBistro, where he is managing the sales and marketing strategy, support teams and partnership programs for the region. He brings with him a wealth of knowledge and experience of the Latin American market as both a business owner of a travel agency and Senior Manager for Booking.com. He understands the challenges of working within this region as well as the enormous opportunity it represents.

Since making the move to Canada from Argentina, he has worked with the LATAM team at eBay and successfully launched Vend into the region before taking on the role at TouchBistro where they have seen rapid success in their expansion in the region.

Jonatan was Awarded "Best Young Entrepreneur" by the IAE of Buenos Aires in 2010.
