Sergio Armenta

Nxtview

Sergio works as a Project Manager in Nxtview, interconnecting thousands of consumption sites to the Mexican electrical network through smart metering. Nevertheless, his true drive is towards creating a more sustainable world with the aid of technology and the internet of things.

Some other activities involve Operational Manager at Nxtview Iot and support to IOTNET MX in order to deploy the SIGFOX network at a faster pace.

Sergio holds a BS from the Monterrey Institute of Technology and Higher Education as a Mechatronics Engineer and has a MSc from the Eindhoven University of Technology in Sustainable Energy Technologies specializing in the built environment and smart grids.
