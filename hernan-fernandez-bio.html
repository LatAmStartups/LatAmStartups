Hérnan Fernández

Managing Director, Angel Ventures México

Hérnan Fernández is the managing director of Angel Ventures México

He developed his professional experience at the strategic consulting firm Booz & Co. as a business consultant, and at PWC, as a corporate lawyer in their offices in Mexico and France. While pursuing his Master's Degree, Hérnan worked as a consultant for the United Nations Program for sustainable development (UNDP-GSB) in New York and in Paraguay, and also for Tecnonexo, an Endeavor company in Argentina.

He has received more than 3,000 investment proposals, resulting in 700 business plans, 150 investment offers, 100 project presentations, of which 17 have resulted in a total investment of $12 million. Between 2013 and 2014, Angel Ventures has expanded into the United States, Colombia and Peru, and began operating an AVM-I co-investment fund, and has co-invested more than $20 million in 18 different companies as of the end of 2015.

Hérnan holds an MBA from the MIT Sloan School of Management and is a practicing lawyer in Mexico.
