LatAm Startups has reached an agreement with the hotel City Express El Angel in Mexico City to host the many conference attendees visiting from out of town.

Prices start at $1463 pesos, roughly USD $77, and include breakfast, internet, gym access, and taxes.

Spaces are limited, we recommend reserving your room as soon as your travel plans are confirmed. (And have you grabbed your conference tickets, yet? Early Bird ends soon...just sayin.)

To get the special conference group rate, call 01800 248 9397 (in Mexico) or from the USA and Canada (+1 855 222 8285) and mention our discount code "100661."
