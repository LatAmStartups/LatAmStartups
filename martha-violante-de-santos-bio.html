Martha Violante de Santos

Editor-in-Chief, Entrepreneur.com en español

Business journalist born in Mexico City. Alumni of the School of Communications of Universidad Panamericana, she has specialized in digital media, working for diverse news websites focused on business information such as Mundo Ejecutivo and Alto Nivel. She has worked with important journalist in Mexico, with the likes of Pedro Ferriz de Con and Genaro Mejía.

Also interested in scientific, technological and historical divulgation, Martha Elena was editor in chief of the page Cómo Funciona and has collaborated with the digital version of México Desconocido.

Her personal passion for movies and comic books have made her work with pages like Cine Premiere and Tomatazos (Rotten Tomatoes Latam).

She currently works as editor in chief in Entrepreneur.com, the biggest site in Latin America for small businesses.
