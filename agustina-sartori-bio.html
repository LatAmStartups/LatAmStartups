Agustina Sartori

Founder & CEO, GlamST

Agustina Sartori is Founder and CEO of GlamST, a software company focused on creating virtual makeover solutions combining augmented reality, artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies. GlamST was acquired by the biggest beauty retail chain in United States, Ulta Beauty in November 2018, the team is now part of Digital Innovation at Ulta Beauty and Agustina is Director of AR Innovation. Agustina is originally from Uruguay, she received her Telematics Engineering degree from the University of Montevideo and presently resides in San Francisco, where she remotely oversees the technology team in Uruguay. Agustina was responsible for fundraising to accelerate GlamST’s growth, closing +2M in capital from investors in Silicon Valley and around the globe. GlamST was supported by ANII, Uruguay XXI, Startup Chile, 500 Startups and Plug and Play. She grew her client base in the US to include big name brands such as Ulta Beauty, Maybelline, Clarins, Dufry and Estee Lauder. Agustina has been awarded several international prizes and recognitions, chosen by Fortune Magazine and the US State Department for the Fortune’s Most Powerful Women Global Mentorship Partnership, received a scholarship for the Stanford Latino Entrepreneurship program and accepted in exclusive organizations such as Endeavor. She serves as volunteer, judge and speaker/mentor in several entrepreneurship organizations such as AIESEC, University of Montevideo, Beauty Tech SF, ICT4V, Startup Chile, 500 Startups, Bay Brazil SF, Latinas in Tech San Francisco, and Microsoft.
