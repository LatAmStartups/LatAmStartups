Investors

Looking to invest in Latin America startups?

If you're an angel investor, you know how hard it is to find startups worth investing in. Too much money chasing too little value. Except...

Latin America Needs You

Across the region and around the world, the cost of innovation is dropping. Startups in Latin America are engaged in world-beating scientific research and innovation, but lack the experience to commercialize their businesses. And because most successful LatAm startup founders wind up in Silicon Valley, the LatAm ecosystems lack the self-sustaining cycle of successful startup founders turned angel investors who invest in new startups to repeat the cycle all over again.

With 800 million consumers and maturing economies taking their place at the First World table, you can't afford to ignore Latin America any longer.
