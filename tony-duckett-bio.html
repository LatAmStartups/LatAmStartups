Tony Duckett

Angel Investor, xiVENTURES Fund

Tony Duckett has 40+ years in leadership roles in the Telecommunications, software development and consulting industries. He also has extensive international experience, having lived and worked in 6 countries and 5 continents. His current focus (as Managing Director of xiBOSS Corporation) is scaling their “Growth Services for Early-Mid Stage Companies” division. Tony is also a Director of xiVENTURES Fund Inc., a Toronto based boutique venture fund.

xiBOSS Corporation is a talent procurement, business consulting, and start-up advisory firm based in Toronto.

The xiBOSS division “Growth Services for Early/Mid Stage Companies” provides services focused on:

  • Talent
  • Business Guidance
  • Software Development

Since 2002 xiBOSS has been called on to help clients in Canada and 27 other countries, in a number of industry sectors including Finance, Telecommunications, Utilities, Media, Facilities Management and Health. xiBOSS customers range in size from start-ups to enterprise level multi-nationals.

xiBOSS has been recognized in 2016 on the Branham 300 list of top Canadian ICT Companies
