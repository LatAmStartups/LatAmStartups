Rob Douglas

Angel Investor

NACO

A chartered accountant by profession, Rob Douglas is distinguished by his strong entrepreneurial spirit and his wealth of experience in owning and managing private businesses. Mr. Douglas’ business career spans more than three decades, with employment experience ranging from investment counselling with a major Canadian brokerage to senior financial management with a large, multi-plant manufacturing company.

Since 1978, Mr. Douglas has pursued interests as an owner and partner in several successful private businesses. With overall responsibility for managing businesses, he has developed strong skills in strategic planning, business plan execution, process analysis and human resource development.

As an Angel investor, Mr. Douglas currently holds equity positions in several early-stage growth companies. He provides management services to these organizations and sits as a member of their boards of directors. In addition to his personal Angel activities, Rob is a founder and President of the Golden Triangle Angel Network (GTAN), one of Canada’s largest and most active Angel investor networks.
