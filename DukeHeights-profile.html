Duke Heights Business Improvement Area (BIA)

DUKE Heights Business Improvement Area (BIA) is the second largest business improvement area in North America and is one of the last significant employment areas in the City of Toronto with room for growth in manufacturing, retail, and office space. Over 32,000 people work in DUKE Heights and 34,500 visit out BIA every work day. In the last 10 years, there has been a steady increase in the number of businesses moving to DUKE Heights. With over 2,500 businesses operating in all of the major sectors of the economy, DUKE Heights is thriving. The new Finch West Subway station, multiple GO train stations and the future Finch LRT under development, it is one the most well-connected areas of the GTA. There are over 1,819 acres of land within our BIA and is bordered by Steeles Avenue on the North, Dufferin Street on the East, Sheppard Avenue on the South and Keele Street on the West.
