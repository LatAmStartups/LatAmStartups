Luis Miguel Beristain

Director, Entrepreneurship Institute, Mexico City

Luis Miguel Beristain is a scholar and entrepreneur. He has established over twenty companies throughout his career and supported more than five hundred entrepreneurs in achieving their vision of the Entrepreneurship Division and Parque Tecnológico en Ciencas para la Vida del Campus Ciudad de México del Tecnológico de Monterrey. Currently, he is the Director of the Entrepreneurship Institute Eugenio Garza la Güera of the Metropolitan Area of Mexico City. He has worked as the Director of the Division of Health Sciences, Director of Academic Support, Director of Entailment, Director of Administration and Finance, and the Director of the Division and Managing Director of the Central Campus of Veracruz.

He has a PhD in Administrative Sciences from the Tecnológico de Monterrey, a Master’s degree in Business Administration from Instituto Panamericano de Alta Dirección de Empresas, and he is a graduate in International Relations from Universidad de las Américas Puebla.
