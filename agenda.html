LatAm Startups Conference — Agenda 2016

October 12-14, 2016

Two intense days of workshops, presentations and networking—plus an optional tour of the booming Mexico City startup ecosystem.

October 12

Optional Tour of the Mexico City Startup Ecosystem

Mexico City has an amazing startup ecosystem. Come check out the city on our optional day tour of the best accelerators in Mexico City!

Day ends with a complementary welcome reception at Tec Monterrey.

Scaling Your Latin American Startup Into North America (The Canadian Way)

Three Verticals (Afternoon Workshops):

  • * Opportunities in the Energy Sector
  • * Opportunities in the Fintech Sector
  • * Opportunities in IoT (Internet of Things)

Conference ends with a tequila-fueled founders party--don't miss it!
