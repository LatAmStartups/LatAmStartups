Joss Monzon

Creative Director, VirtualWare

Over the past twenty years, Joss Monzon has led creative and strategic teams all over the world working in physical and digital playgrounds across lifestyle, entertainment, technology and non-profits, with a unique philosophy: to make beautiful simple work, that cuts through the noise and helps brands tell their stories. Today as Creative Director of Virtualware, Joss Monzon has been instrumental in finding the ingredients that work at the intersection between technology, art and storytelling.
