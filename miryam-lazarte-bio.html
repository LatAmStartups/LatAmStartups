Miryam Lazarte

Co-founder & CEO, LatAm Startups

Miryam is a pioneer connecting the Canadian and Latin American innovation markets. In addition to serving as CEO at LatAm Startups, Miryam is also Vice-President, Business Development, of Go South! Consulting Inc. Go South! helps Canadian high-tech companies scale into the booming Latin American markets.

Miryam has more than twenty years of experience in business development, with a focus on Latin American markets. She is passionate about connecting the Canadian and Latin American startup ecosystems.
