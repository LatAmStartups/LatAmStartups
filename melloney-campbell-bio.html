Melloney Campbell

CEO, Startup Peel

Melloney Campbell is a professional working in digital media for over 16 years with experience in Broadcast and Interactive Media Platforms and a strong supporter of entrepreneurs. It was her support for entrepreneurship that brought her to Startup Canada where she held the role of VP of Communications managing the national program consisting of Social Media Channels, Sponsor and Partner Relations in addition to planning and managing Entrepreneurship events. After her tenure as VP of Communications, Melloney has taken on the volunteer role of Startup Community Lead for the Region of Peel. Startup Peel became an official Startup Canada Community in December of 2015 with the mandate of showcasing the work of entrepreneurs in the community to the rest of the world.
