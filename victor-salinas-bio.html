Victor Salinas

CEO AionTech

Victor Salinas has been working in the financial industry for more than 20 years designing and implementing solutions for domestic and international payments, and also, implementing solutions for bank reconciliation process and money market. His goal is to develop competitive technologies at a global ad innovative level.

As CEO of Aiontech, Victor has participated in projects and expanded into new markets not just for financial institutions but big companies and corporations. Aiontech solutions are focused to guide CFO's and CTO's to strategic making decisions.
