Peter Elkins

Angel Investor

An accomplished mentor, advisor and entrepreneur with start-up, growth, and enterprise acumen. Peter is the founder and CEO of a public safety distribution company based in Vancouver, B.C., and a Fortune 500 management consultant specializing in performance acceleration and crisis management with assignments throughout the Americas and Europe.

Currently, Peter is very active in community economic development in western Canada and has several projects in the works to support the future of local living economies, including Imagination Funds, Entrepreneurship Victoria, and Impact HUB Victoria. He also organizes Meet Ups for aspiring social entrepreneurs, and he recently launched Kick Victoria, a brand new initiative in partnership with Seattle Kick, that provides entrepreneurial education and a portable inclusive “impact business” incubator to support both entrepreneurs and intrepreneurs.

A compassionate, mindful entrepreneur who thrives on the services he provides, Peter will assess your company and find trapped value and business development opportunities through the rapid implementation of high value strategies.
