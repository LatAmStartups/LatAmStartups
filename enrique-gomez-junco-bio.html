Enrique Gómez Junco

Founder & CEO, Optima Energía

Enrique Gómez Junco is a chemical engineer graduated from Tec de Monterrey with a specialization in Management. When Enrique was 22 years old he created Optima Energia, through the years he has been the CEO of this company being the leader of the efficiency energy solutions in the Latin American market.

Some of the recognition in the market are:

  • * First Latin American company in the final competion at Bloomber New Energy Finance (BNEF) NY 2015
  • * Recognition by IMPULSA Roberto Segovia Kane 213
  • * Award Wharton-Infosys Business Transformation 2006
  • * Entrepreneur of the year, Expansion magazine (Feb 2000 and Sept 2005)
  • * Entrepreneur Endevor (July 2005)
  • * Named one of the global leaders of the future by The Economic World Forum Davos (2001 and 2000)
  • * Latin American leader of the millennium by Times Magazine and CNN (April 1999)

Other activities:

  • * Member and speaker of the World Forum
  • * Speaker for the G20 (Aby and Sevilla 2011)
  • * Speaker at the ITESM, IBERO, UDEM, WHARTON, BASON SCHOOL, CHICAGO U, MIT between others.
  • * Adviser and president for the NGO unido lo lograremos A.C. (2005-2009)
  • * founder and adviser of Enlace E+E
  • * Adviser at Tec de monterrrey
  • * President of the board of advisers at ENDEVOR Noreste
  • * Member of the administration council of CARITAS de Monterrey

Optima Energía (OE) is a finance vehicle that offers energy efficient solutions to hotels, industry, and Mexican municipalities. The company has completed more than 120 turn-key solutions. OE provides solutions under the model of "contract for results" to develop sustainable infrastructure for Mexican municipalities.
