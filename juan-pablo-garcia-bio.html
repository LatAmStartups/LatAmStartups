Juan Pablo Garcia Aguilar

Co-founder, Vetelia

Juan Pablo is the co-founder of Vetelia, a design shop and electronic vehicle manufacturer in Leon, Guanajuato. Vetelia has recently integrated IoT ("Internet of Things") components into their line of electric bicycles.

During the last four years, Juan Pablo has dedicated himself to changing how we get around our cities. He holds an engineering degree from the Universidad Iberoamericana Leon, loves fast cars and mechanical design.
