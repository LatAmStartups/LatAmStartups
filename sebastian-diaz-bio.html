Sebastián Andrés Díaz Mesa

Start-Up Chile

Sebastián Díaz began his career at Start-Up Chile in June of 2014 as Manager of Communications and Public Relations, and is now the program's Director of Marketing and Communications.

He has been in charge of Start-Up Chile's strategic marketing and communications, development and maintenance of the blog, and supervised the program's social media and video editing. He also create SUP News, another news channel for the program.

In the past, Díaz's career as a journalist included experience at Cadem Research, working as head of press and community manager, as editor at I Go Chile, a monthly managize that covered trends, travel, culture, and outdoor sports, and also his work as a journalist and anchor at Emol.com, the online version of the daily El Mercurio and one of the most visited news web sites in Chile.

Previously, Díaz worked as a journalist and editor in television, radio, and print.
