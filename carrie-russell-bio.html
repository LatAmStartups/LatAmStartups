Carrie Russell

Market and Product Lead, Finn AI

Carrie Russell is an innovative business line leader, product/solution designer, and renowned builder of consumer and B2B brands in Financial Services and Technology Solutions companies. Former senior executive leading retail, commercial and wealth management business lines at TD Bank, and CMO at D+H, one of Canada’s largest Fintech companies, Carrie has witnessed and steered the digital transformation from the front seat. She is a pioneer in leading innovation, creating thought leadership, and implementing experience design strategies to drive consumer relevance, employee engagement and accelerate growth.

Today Carrie is the Market and Product Lead at Finn AI, an award winning AI-powered virtual assistant built for personal banking and finance, deployed in partnership with Banks and Credit Unions globally. Carrie works along-side founders, Nat Cartwright and Jake Tyler to identify, plan and manage the new capabilities, organizational change, and market engagement approaches required to succeed and win in the new “experience economy”. She speaks regularly at industry and private workshops on value proposition design, customer centred growth, and the power of design thinking in creating breakthrough commercialization strategies. Carrie lives in Toronto with her 2 sons and husband, but calls Duntroon, Ontario her authentic home.
