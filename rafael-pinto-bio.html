Rafael Pinto

CEO, Occasio International

As CEO of Rio-based Occasio, Rafael focuses on helping high-tech foreign companies enter the complex and challenging Brazilian market.

Rafael has managed several projects that involved multi-stakeholder participation, including International Organizations, non-governmental organizations, universities, and government agencies. With over 14 years of experience in the design, implementation and expansion of large-scale regional programs in Latin America and the Caribbean, Asia and the United States. He also has extensive experience in developing successful training programs. Trilingual (Portuguese, Spanish, and English).

He has helped organize and has participated as a speaker in several international conferences focused on entrepreneurship related topics.
