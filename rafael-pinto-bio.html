Rafael Pinto

CEO, Occasio International

Rafael Pinto has over 15 years of experience working in the design, implementation and evaluation of projects in the fields of Entrepreneurship, Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), Business Development, Public-Private Partnerships, and other areas. He has developed several projects that involved multi-stakeholder participation including International Organizations, non-governmental organizations, the private sector, universities, and government agencies. He has worked in organizations such as the Young Americas Business Trust - of the Organization of American States, the International Finance Corporation of the World Bank Group, and the United Nations Association of the Dominican Republic.

He is now the Founder and Director of Occasio International Advisory Services (www.occasioias.com), which brings together professionals with experiences working in Brazil and internationally. Working closely with partners in its extensive network, Occasio offers business development opportunities and support of new and ongoing innovative projects. Rafael is a member of the Business Association of Rio de Janeiro, and he speaks Portuguese, Spanish and English fluently.
