Roger Vandomme

Chief Data Scientist and Principal, SMC Inc

Roger’s career has been built on the fundamentals of data analysis, predictive modeling and related decision-making.

Roger has an outstanding unmatched skill-set in the field of predictive modeling and has completed numerous studies and research on decision heuristics and biases, developing reasoning methods and processes around systemic design and game theory.

With a 20 year experience in credit scoring, Roger has been involved in most of recent Fintech credit adjudication automation engines.

Roger holds a Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics from Paris University, a MBA from Queen’s University, and a Master in Defense Studies with the Royal Canadian Military College.
