uGlobally

uGlobally.com

uGlobally is an online platform that connects innovative companies with international opportunities around the world

uGlobally is a SaaS all-in-one internationalization platform that helps innovative entrepreneurs take their business abroad. Startups and tech companies can benefit from a complete set of tools specially designed to transform your local business into a global company. uGlobally members can get access to an incredible range of programs, from pre-accelerators to exclusive soft-landing experiences, events, tools, articles, videos and much more.

uGlobally is a company 100% focused on finding the best opportunities to take startups and tech businesses to new markets. On our platform, we only have programs that accept international companies in their batch.

The majority of our soft-landing programs are exclusive--we’ve co-created it with major organizations, always focusing on the startup’s needs when going global.

We also offer tailored immersion programs and trade missions around the world, everything is done thinking in connecting you with the maximum opportunities to boost your business to an international level.

Institutions such as accelerators, coworking spaces, governments, incubators, and universities are invited to share their global opportunities on uGlobally and attract entrepreneurs from all over the world to their countries
