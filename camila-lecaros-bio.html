Camila Lecaros

Managing Director, MassChallenge Mexico

Camila Lecaros is Managing Director of MassChallenge Mexico, a global network of accelerators in Boston, London, Mexico City, Switzerland and Israel, providing startups with the resources they need to competing globally. Camila is a leader in the entrepreneurial and investment scenes in South America. Previously, she was a partner with Nazca Ventures, a $20 million fund in Bogota, Colombia, and headed the Outreach area at Endeavor Colombia.

Camila also worked with entrepreneurs at Agora Partnerships in Nicaragua. Most recently she was the Entrepreneur in Residence with All Venture Partners in Mexico City.

She is an industrial engineer from Universidad de los Andes in Colombia and holds a Master in Public Administration from Columbia University in New York.
