Guadalupe Itzel Villa

INADEM

Itzel Villa is currently the General Director of the Entrepreneurship Programs and Financing at the National Institute of Entrepreneur of the Ministry of Economy. She is also involved in initiatives with the entrepreneurial ecosystem at national and international level.

She began her career in the private sector doing market research of financial institutions, later on, she joined in 2008 NAFIN (Nacional Financiera) to be involved with the platform for angel investors. She has evaluated and linked more than 500 business of high impact projects. She helped to design and promote the capital development strategy in this institution.

In 2014, Itzel Villa participated in the leader emerging program “Mexican Innovative Entrepreneurship and technology transfer” from the Department of the State. She has also participated at the SME Policy Sharing Program in South Korea. Also, she was in charge of the High Impact Direction at INADEM until 2016 and she serves as the representative of Mexico Government in the Community Startup Nations from the Global Entrepreneurship Network (GEN).

She has bachelor's degree in Business Relationships from the National Polytechnic Institute (IPN) and has a certification in creative leadership and innovation facilitation by the Creativity Certification Center of the University of Buffalo, N.Y.
