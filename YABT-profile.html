YABT

Young Americas Business Trust

The Young Americas Business Trust (YABT) is a nonprofit organization that works in cooperation with the Organization of American States (OAS) to promote social and economic development of young people around the world. While building entrepreneurial capacity and creating opportunities for future business leaders, the YABT community advances the global goals and strengthens youth-led initiatives for sustainable development. YABT has four focus areas to support youth ventures, especially those working on poverty reduction, employment, climate change, peace and democracy.
