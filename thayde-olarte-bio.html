Thayde Olarte

VP Fintech Partnership

Scotiabank

Thayde Olarte has more than 20 years of retail, commercial and corporate banking experience. She joined Scotiabank in 2014 as Risk Management Director in GRM, with responsibility for Peru and CMF. She also successfully managed governance and oversight over CMF portfolios in IB and led implementation of best practices across the markets.

In prior work, Thayde has been working in revolving and unsecured credit for a large Financial services company in Canada. Also, she has played lead roles for global banks located in Latin America by developing and executing marketing, product and channel strategy in the Venezuelan and Andean region, through progressively senior positions within the Financial services industry.

During her banking experience, she has been involved in Diversity, Women Mentorship and Community programs, by influencing and fostering inclusive culture across the teams.

Thayde holds a Bachelor degree in Economics from Universidad Central de Venezuela and a Master of Business Administration degree in Finance Management from Universidad Metropolitana (UNIMET) in Venezuela.
