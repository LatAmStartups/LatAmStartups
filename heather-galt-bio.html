Heather Galt

Vice President, Talent Initiatives and an Executive in Residence at Communitech

Heather Galt is the Vice President, Talent Initiatives and an Executive in Residence at Communitech Corp in Waterloo, Ontario. Heather has an extensive background in product and service go-to-market, B2B and channel marketing, and internationalization in small, rapidly scaling, and large companies.

Heather was most recently the VP Marketing at Kik Interactive. Prior to Kik, Heather spent 11 years at BlackBerry in various marketing and channel-facing roles. Heather started her career in start-ups, working in marketing and product management for two rapidly growing companies.

Heather is also a part-time lecturer in marketing for Wilfrid Laurier University's MBA program.

Heather has her MBA in Marketing and Strategy from York University.
