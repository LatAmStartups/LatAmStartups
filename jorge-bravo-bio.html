Jorge Bravo

Legal Director, Pacific Alliance Chamber of Commerce

Profile:
- Legal Director of the of the Pacific Alliance’s Chamber of Commerce.
- Lawyer by profession, with a master’s in Commercial Law and a PhD from
the University of Zaragoza.
- Experience in Civil Litigation and Corporate Commercial Law.
- Member of the Madrid law firm, Life Abogados.
- University professor of introductory law courses and commercial law.
- Founder of Luma Consulting Group, a company dedicated to the
internationalization of companies and the beginning of operations in Peru.
- Specialist in public procurement and formulation of Public-Private
Partnerships (PPP).

Teaching, publications and lectures:
He has been teaching for over 13 years and, due to his work in promoting
internationalization, he spends a lot of his working life traveling and
participating in workshops and conferences in countries throughout Latin
America and Europe.

Member of:
Member of the Order of the Lima Bar Association.
Founding associate of Compañía de las Obras - CDO Perú.
Director of the Pacific Alliance’s Chamber of Commerce.
